Site Name

Fremer Transportation Company

The name clearly identifies a professional local transportation service while using “Fremer” as a memorable, distinctive brand name. The logo’s road-inspired F reinforces the company’s focus on moving people safely and efficiently.

Optional domain: fremertransport.com

Site Purpose

Fremer Transportation Company will provide a simple online hub for passengers to learn about local taxi services, estimate fares, book rides, and submit payment details. It will also give drivers a clear way to review assigned trips and completed-ride receipts. The site will replace manual booking records with an organized, mobile-friendly experience.

Scenarios

Passenger

“I need a ride from the city center to the airport. What will the trip cost, and can I pay with Mobile Money?”

Driver

“Where can I see my assigned trips for today and confirm the receipt for a completed ride?”

Typography

Montserrat

Use: Headings, navigation, buttons, and important labels. Its bold, clean letterforms make information easy to scan.

Open Sans

Use: Paragraphs, form labels, and general content. It remains legible on mobile screens and at smaller sizes.
